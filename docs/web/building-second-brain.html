Building Second Brain - Tiago Forte

Having a second brain is about offloading information from brain to a commonplace. When need arise in future information can be referred from commonplace without relying on brain to remember every single detail which is a tiring process.

 

Building second brain has four main steps having acronym CODE

  1. C for Capture:
    • capture not too much and not too little. Too much information is no better than reading entire thing and overwhelm the future brain.
    • There are four main capture criteria
      • U for Useful: Capture whats is useful
      • P for Personal: Capture personal thoughts, ideas, memories.
      • I for Inspiring: Capture what inspire you
      • S for Surprising: Capture what is surprising for you.
    • For every resource you want to capture, add a link to original source. And add essence of the material which is no longer than few sentences.
