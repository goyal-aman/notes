Short Note: How to win friends and influence people

  • Praise in more effective than criticism
  • You will have way more success in dealing with people when you make them feel important and interesting
    • Stop trying to make yourself interesting 
    • Show your genuine interest in other people
    • Show people you are happy with them and feel them important
  • Encourage others to talk and listen when they do
    • Other people appreciate people who are listen
    • Invite other people to talk about them
    • Ask people open ended question
      • How are you fitting in your new role
      • How is your current role different from past
  • If you can’t avoid an agreement- then disagree as gently as possible
    • if your opponent resents you - they will never agree with you
    • when disagreements become full blown arguments - no one wins
    • If you want to convince someone they are wrong
      • never say you are wrong
      • try to lead your opponent to your conclusion - try by saying this “I might be wrong .. but this is why I believe so ..“
      • if you are wrong accept as gentle person
      • if you are right - don’t enjoy
    • If you can get people to nodding from the beginning, you are halfway there to convincing people you are right. Avoid no - people become emotionally invested in defending which causes them to disagree.
    • people prefer ideas which they come up with rather than someone else’s
      • instead to shoving your ideas on others lead them to come to your conclusions on their own
  • Trying to see situation from other people POV is always helpful.
    • People like to be understood
    • Often saying the words “I understand what you are coming from .. “
    • Extend sympathy is better than getting angry for the situation and you and other people.
    • By understanding the factors that make people act as they do you can become more tolerant of behaviors that once irritated and upset you.
  • Set the bar high - and people will strive to meet it.
  • When we praise someone they feel rewarded and it also set the bar high for them to meet.
    • Set up a aspirational reputation
    • If you want someone to possess certain characteristics- speak to them as they already poses it.

Conclusion

  • Becoming truly influential involves trying to understand the perspective of those around you
  • Everybody likes to be listened to, understood, and made to feel important.
  • If you can do these things for other people you will always find favor.
