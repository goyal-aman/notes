Multi-Column Index

Multi-Column index or Multi-Dimensional index are used to narrow down searches based on multiple columns. Single dimensional indexes which are based on b-trees can narrow down searches on single field at a time.

In a weather database observations you could have a two-dimensional index on (date, temperature) in order to efficiently search for all the observations during the year 2013 where the temperature was between 25 and 30Â°C. With a one- dimensional index, you would have to either scan over all the records from 2013 (regardless of temperature) and then filter them by temperature, or vice versa.
